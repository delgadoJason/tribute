Harry "Handcuff King" Houdini

Born Ehrich Weisz

Here's a timeline of Harry Houdini's Life

  • March 24, 1874 - Harry was born as Ehrich Weiss in Budapest, Hungary
  • 1878 - Ehrich's family moves to Wisconsin
  • 1883 - At age 9, Ehrich puts on his first show in a circus. He called himself the "Prince of Air"
  • 1891 - Ehrich moves with his father to New York, and started calling himself Harry Houdini
  • 1893 - Harry joins up with a friend, and they called themselves the "Brothers Houdini." Their first big show was in Chicago
  • 1893 - Harry's friend eventually stops performing, and he was later replaced with Bess. Bess and Harry get married, and they call the show, "The Houdinis"
  • 1895 - The Houdinis gain fame. Their first famous trick was where they traded places inside of a locked trunk. Harry was also escaping from handcuffs at this time
  • 1898 - Harry starts up a business called Harry Houdini's school of magic
  • 1899 - Houdini catches his first big break when a theatre manager asked Harry to start working for him
  • 1900 - Houdini travels to England and then Germany. He became famous overseas
  • 1904 - A blacksmith in England took 5 years to make an unescapable set of handcuffs. It only took Houdini 1 hour to break free
  • 1906 - Harry Houdini escapes out of a prison cell in Washington D.C.
  • 1907 - Houdini escapes after being handcuffed and chained, and jumping from bridge
  • 1926 - Houdini dies in Detroit after a show from appendicitis
